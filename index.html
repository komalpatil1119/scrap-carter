about us

Protecting nature nurturing a sustainable future

Scrap Carter is a smart scrap collection service helping you turn waste into impact. We offer doorstep pickups, transparent pricing, and instant payments for your scrap. Our mission is to create a cleaner environment and support local jobs, especially for women in scrap segregation and recycling.

Join us in building a Zero Waste Society and a greener future.

Our Mission

To revolutionize scrap collection by offering smart, transparent, and eco-friendly solutions that empower individuals, housing societies, and businesses to manage waste responsibly. We aim to create impact through technology, sustainability, and trust.

Our Vision

To build a Zero Waste Society where every scrap is reused, every citizen is aware, and every action contributes to a greener, cleaner, and more sustainable future for all.
