about us

Protecting nature nurturing a sustainable future

Scrap Carter is a smart scrap collection and recycling service on a mission to revolutionize how households, businesses, and societies manage their waste with ease, transparency, and purpose.

Through doorstep pickups, fair pricing, and instant payments, we offer a seamless and rewarding experience for our customers.

We are more than just a scrap pickup service. We are a social impact movement. By employing local workers, especially women for segregation and recycling work, we contribute to community empowerment and sustainable employment.

Join us in building a greener future and a Zero Waste Society.

Our Mission

To revolutionize scrap collection by offering smart, transparent, and eco-friendly solutions that empower individuals, housing societies, and businesses to manage waste responsibly. We aim to create impact through technology, sustainability, and trust.

Our Vision

To build a Zero Waste Society where every scrap is reused, every citizen is aware, and every action contributes to a greener, cleaner, and more sustainable future for all.

We collect dry recyclable waste such as steel, iron, copper and aluminium. Wet waste, hazardous waste, or bio-waste are not accepted.

You can schedule a pickup through our website or mobile app. Simply choose your location, date, and time that suits you best.

We offer transparent, standard pricing based on the type and weight of the scrap. Prices are updated regularly and visible on our platform.

We accept all quantities, but for very small amounts, we may schedule pickups on specific days or club multiple requests to optimize service.
